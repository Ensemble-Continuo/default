Audition process

Send us a message below to introduce yourself and mention your singing experience and we will get back to you with an audition time.

An audition takes about 10 minutes:

  1. We will start with a range check to warm up your voice.
  2. Then we will sing one prepared piece of your choice. Anything you are comfortable with, for example a part of a choral work you know, an aria, or an art song of your choice. This should be not longer than 2 minutes.
  3. The audition ends with sight-reading a simple melody.

Our rehearsals are Tuesdays 5:30-7:30pm at Santiago Villa, Space Park Way, Mountain View. Afterwards we usually grab dinner at a nearby cafe.
